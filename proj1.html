LUNAR LANDER 2023

The Lunar Lander Challenge involves building and flying a rocket-powered vehicle that simulates the flight of a vehicle on the Moon. The lander must take off vertically then travel horizontally and land accurately at another spot. Then the same vehicle must take off again, travel horizontally back to the original take off point and land successfully. The prize purse is divided into first and second prizes for Level One and Level Two, which require a flight duration of at least 90 seconds on each flight and 180 seconds, respectively. Furthermore, one of the landings for a Level Two attempt must be made on a simulated lunar terrain with rocks and craters.
